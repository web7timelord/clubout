what we do

We craft enhanced user experiences for innovative hospitality venues

novel experiences

  • Loyalty Program

    A SaaS platform for Pubs, Bars, Clubs & Lounges to facilitate tiered loyalty system offering personalized rewards to their customers. We enable on-chain loyalty token to be issued as loyalty rewards.

  • Dynamic Pricing Model

    An AI-powered tool that conducts real-time analytics and data-driven decision-making for PBCL. Additionally, this tool can analyze customer behaviour & preferences, enabling the implementation of personalized pricing strategies.

  • Automated Web Check-ins

    We enable seamless web check-ins for tipplers using ZK-KYC technology enhancing their customer experience of visiting any partner PBCL.

pragmatic technologies

  • Payments Infra

    Our customized VISA card enables seamless payments via crypto at any of our partner pubs, bars, clubs & lounges. Tipplers get to earn loyalty rewards whenever they make payments from our VISA card or through in-app via UPI or other payment methods.

  • Mobile Dapp

    An app for tipplers to manage all memberships and claim various loyalty points earned from one place. One can join events listed on our platform and join their respective token-gated chats through our app itself.

  • In-app Marketplace

    Our marketplace let tipplers take an exit from currently subscribed memberships and opt new ones. This brings versatility in clubbing experience for all the tipplers and simultaneously provides fair opportunity to upcoming PBCL venues.
